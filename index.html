Jasper Zhu

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, intending to graduate with majors in Statistics and Combinatorics & Optimization (CO), and a minor in Pure Mathematics.

Most recently, I was a database researcher and developer co-op at SAP Canada under the supervision of Dr. Anisoara Nica developing tools for incremental maintenance of materialized views. I have also previously conducted research with Professors Mingbin (Ben) Feng and Joseph Cheriyan at the University of Waterloo.

In my spare time, I love to play ultimate frisbee and basketball, though I have taken a recent interest in chess as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
